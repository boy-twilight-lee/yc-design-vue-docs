### 竖直分割线

指定 <yc-tag>direction</yc-tag> 为 <yc-tag>vertical</yc-tag> 即可使用竖直分割线。竖直分割线不能带文字。

<div class="cell-demo">
  <div class="divider-demo">
    <span>Item 1</span>
    <yc-divider direction="vertical" />
    <span>Item 2</span>
    <yc-divider direction="vertical" />
    <span>Item 3</span>
  </div>
</div>

<details>
<summary>查看/隐藏代码</summary>

```vue
<template>
  <div class="divider-demo">
    <span>Item 1</span>
    <yc-divider direction="vertical" />
    <span>Item 2</span>
    <yc-divider direction="vertical" />
    <span>Item 3</span>
  </div>
</template>
```

</details>
