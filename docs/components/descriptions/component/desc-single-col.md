### 单列样式

单列的描述列表样式。

<div class="cell-demo">
  <yc-radio-group
    type="button"
    v-model="size">
    <yc-radio value="mini">mini</yc-radio>
    <yc-radio value="small">small</yc-radio>
    <yc-radio value="medium">medium</yc-radio>
    <yc-radio value="large">large</yc-radio>
  </yc-radio-group>
  <yc-descriptions
    style="margin-top: 20px"
    :data="data"
    :size="size"
    title="User Info"
    :column="1" />
</div>
