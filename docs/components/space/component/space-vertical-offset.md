### 垂直间距

可以设置垂直方向排列的间距。

<div class="cell-demo vp-raw">
  <yc-space direction="vertical" fill>
    <yc-button type="primary" long>Item1</yc-button>
    <yc-button type="primary" long>Item2</yc-button>
    <yc-button type="primary" long>Item3</yc-button>
  </yc-space>
</div>
